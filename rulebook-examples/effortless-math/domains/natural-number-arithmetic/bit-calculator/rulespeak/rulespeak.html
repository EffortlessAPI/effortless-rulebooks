📘 bit-calculator RuleSpeak

A half adder, and nothing else. Two input bits a and b; the DB computes sum = a XOR b and carry = a AND b by LOOKING THEM UP in gate truth tables. No arithmetic is stored. The output bit of every computation is a calculated field the substrate derives from truth-table rows.
