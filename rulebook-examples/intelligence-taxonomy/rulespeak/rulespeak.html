ƒ Taxonomy Class Calculated 4 hops from ground truth string DR-3
In RuleSpeak

The intelligence's taxonomy class is determined by the following priority:

  1. “Generalist” if the total weighted score is at least 350
  2. “Broad” if the total weighted score is at least 220
  3. “Narrow” in all other cases
In English

If total weighted score is at least 350 then "Generalist", otherwise if total weighted score is at least 220 then "Broad", otherwise "Narrow".

As a formula
= IF(Total Weighted Score>=350, "Generalist", IF(Total Weighted Score>=220, "Broad", "Narrow"))
