Why we support Vivli
"We are proud we are able to take this step forward in our data sharing program as part of our ongoing data transparency commitments."
Jenny Petersen, Director, Clinical Trial Disclosure and Transparency at Alnylam Pharmaceuticals
"We’re committed to sharing data as a way to fuel treatment breakthroughs for muscular dystrophy. Vivli makes that possible."
Debra Miller, founder and CEO, CureDuchenne
"During this critical time of public health challenge, we welcome the opportunity to work with a trusted partner."
Jeff Helterbrand, Global Biometrics Head at Roche
"We’ve made an important piece of research and important contribution to the field, which would have been impossible without the sharing of data."
Professor José António P. Da Silva, University of Coimbra
"The mission of Vivli is fully aligned with that of Harvard University in promoting collaborative research and data sharing and transparency."
Ara Tahmassian, Ph.D., Chief Research Compliance Officer at Harvard University
"UCB is proud to support the work that the Vivli team is undertaking to drive collaboration and data sharing, as we believe this will enhance disease understanding, generate new insights, and may help develop new treatments to benefit patients worldwide."
Professor Dr. Iris Loew-Friedrich, chief medical officer and executive vice president, UCB
"Data generated by NIH HEAL holds the key to helping patients and communities devastated by the opioid crisis, as well as those seeking relief for unmanaged chronic pain that limits all aspects of life. Vivli offers a valuable tool for researchers in HEAL and beyond to access this rich set of data stored in various repositories that meet security and privacy standards – giving HEAL researchers a great option to leverage their data for new discovery and long-term preservation."
Rebecca Baker, Ph.D., Director, NIH HEAL Initiative
