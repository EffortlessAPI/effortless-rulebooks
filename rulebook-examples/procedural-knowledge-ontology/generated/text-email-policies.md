# Employee Email and Text-Message Policies

Generated from the same canonical PKO rulebook as the SOP and execution model. PKO profile: https://w3id.org/pko/2.0.0.

## Email

- **Status:** Active
- **Audience:** All affected employees with a valid corporate email
- **Consent required:** No
- **Quiet hours:** 00:00–00:00 recipient-local time
- **Maximum length:** 100000 characters; 1 segment(s)
- **Retention:** 2555 days
- **Approval role:** Employee Communications Manager
- **Required content:** Full policy context, effective date, action required, alternate contact
- **Authority:** Email is the complete official notification record.

### Approved templates

#### en-US

**Subject:** `Remote-work policy update effective {{EffectiveDate}}`

A workforce policy affecting your work has changed. Review the complete policy at {{PolicyUrl}}. Required action: {{RequiredAction}}. Questions: {{ContactAddress}}.

### Governing procedure steps

- **04. Legal and privacy review:** Review legal commitments, privacy, consent, retention, and jurisdictional requirements.
- **05. Human authority approval:** Approve the policy meaning and all channel constraints.
- **06. Generate approved communications:** Render approved email and SMS messages without changing policy meaning.
- **07. Apply channel controls and send:** Enforce consent, quiet hours, opt-out text, recipient scope, and delivery logging.
- **08. Collect acknowledgements and exceptions:** Collect acknowledgements and create exception tasks for unreachable recipients.

## SMS

- **Status:** Active
- **Audience:** Affected employees with active SMS consent
- **Consent required:** Yes
- **Quiet hours:** 20:00–08:00 recipient-local time
- **Maximum length:** 160 characters; 3 segment(s)
- **Retention:** 2555 days
- **Approval role:** Employee Communications Manager
- **Required content:** Short notice, secure link, approved opt-out text
- **Authority:** SMS is a supplemental notice; the linked email/policy is authoritative.
